Our Lab and Developers

  Our biomedical engineering research team led by Professor Kevin Tsia, programme director of the Biomedical Engineering Programme under the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Hong Kong, in collaboration with Professor Joshua Ho and Professor Yuanhua Huang from the Li Ka Shing Faculty of Medicine, has created an all-in-one large-scale computational framework StaVia to uncover the development of cellular trajectories.

  This novel computational tool was developed by our post-doctoral student, Shobana Venkant Stassen, and PhD student, Minato Kobashi. We have successfully applied the tool in analysing the development process of zebrafish embryos. StaVia optimizes the germ layer differentiation analysis in zebrafish development with only the use of Stereo-seq data, thereby integrating spatial coordinates, gene expression, and time-series data for intuitive cartographic analysis and atlas view.
